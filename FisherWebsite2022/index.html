Just Help

Kiosk Web-app

"Just Help" is a web-app based program. It's goal was to be placed in high trafficked area's to give advice and comfort to Opioid sufferers. 

PreviousNext

JustHelp is a tablet sized touch based (button supplemented) interface to be installed in the form of a kiosk. These kiosks would be placed at high traffic public locations in the areas where they are most needed. Focusing on churches, homeless shelters and some businesses for the location. But, placing the kiosks themselves in private secluded spots in those buildings to provide the dignity that this service aims to instate. The kiosks will guide the user through a linear flow based on a plain word questionnaire that would cater results to the user’s desire and end with an actionable recommendation that the user could follow. Offering the option to print information to be taken with the user as they leave the kiosk.

Silly Philly

VR Experience

"Silly Philly" is a VR experience. As project manager and one of the UX designers, I worked with a group of TJU undergrad and grad students. Our aim was to create a marketing tool for local non-profit “Fresh Artists”.

PreviousNext

For this project, was the project manager, one of UX researchers and designed the branding. After meeting extensively with Fresh Artists, we developed a VR experience showing off their student's artwork. This was to be presented to possible investors. I headed our team in collecting research on tangential VR experiences. I was also charged with creating a work schedule for each section of the team. After several months of hard work we were able to deliver a working experience to be used on the Oculus Go, complete with custom assets, animations, and music. Afterwards, we were invited to demonstrate it at Philly Tech Week 2019 and later at a TEDx conference at Temple University.
